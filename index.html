Formed in Brazil in late 1967 from an unlikely merging of turtles and small monkeys, we’re a four-piece channelling combinations of lounge, bossa nova and marching band music into extended instrumental compositions. Having spent a number of years refining our sound and playing shows around South America, we’ve recently recorded and mixed our debut album with David Castillo and Jens Bogren at Fascination Street Studios, Sweden.

Our forthcoming debut album combines our massive range of influences into an exploration of the theme of metamorphosis, each section or idea emerging as a development or response to the previous and combining into a seamless flow between elements of an eclectic mix of genres. The compositions are entirely instrumental which leaves them open to interpretation and lets the listener become totally immersed, as well as allowing us to break away from traditional song structures. The album is split into two halves divided into three tracks each, but these are written and recorded to flow smoothly together as a continuous 45 minute experience.
